    Who are we?

We are a group of Interior Designers. We design personal/commercial interiors for people on a tight budget. We are based in Lucknow. We provide our services all over India. We provide various services. We have been successful in some of our projects. We do design separate office rooms, terrace and not just the complete house. As mentioned earlier, we do from the basics i.e., wood work, marble matching and everything else.

Our practice is Designing Complete Environments—exceptional buildings, communities and places—in special situations, where a unique historic or natural environment requires an especially thoughtful and innovative solution.
